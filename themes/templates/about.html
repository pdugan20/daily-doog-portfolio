Hi! My name is Patrick Dugan and I'm currently a graduate student at the University of Washington pursuing a degree in Human Computer Interaction + Design. I'm also a member of the dub group. Before returning to school I worked at Google in an internal development and data analytics role.

I’m a west-coast transplant via way of Buffalo, NY and I earned my undergraduate degree in Information Systems from the University of Washington.

I’m especially passionate about the impacts of wearable computing devices and have been an avid Google Glass user since late 2012.

In my free time I love to run, swim, bike, camp, and hike. If you’d like to learn more about me, start by checking out my resume, portfolio, or google+ page.
